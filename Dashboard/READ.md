# Real Estate Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive Tableau dashboard for analyzing real estate sales performance across multiple towns, property types, and time periods.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides interactive insights into:

- **Year-over-Year Sales Performance**
- **Top 10 Towns by Total Sales Amount**
- **Distribution of Property Types**
- **Monthly Sales Trends**
- **Dynamic Filtering by Town, Year, and Property Type**

The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders identify market trends, top-performing locations, and property category performance.

## 🚀 Features

### 📈 Year Over Year Sales
- Visualizes annual sales growth from **2011 to 2020**
- Highlights major increases and declines in total sales volume
- Helps identify long-term market trends

### 🏙️ Top 10 Total Sales Amount by Town
- Displays highest-performing towns based on total sales
- Ranked horizontal bar chart for easy comparison
- Enables quick regional performance analysis

### 🏠 Distribution of Top Performing Property Types
- Pie chart representation of property category contribution
- Includes:
  - Single Family
  - Condo
  - Two Family
  - Unknown

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
- Tracks sales fluctuations throughout the year
- Identifies seasonal buying/selling patterns
- Useful for forecasting and planning

### 🎛️ Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter dashboard data by:
- Town
- Year Range
- Property Type

## 📌 Key Insights

- **Single Family properties** dominate the market share with over **70% contribution**
- Significant sales growth observed between **2011–2014**
- Certain towns consistently outperform others in total sales
- Monthly sales peak toward the end of the year
